The Shibuya Incident Arc (渋しぶ谷や事じ変へん編へん Shibuya Jihen-hen?) is the sixth arc in Gege Akutami's Jujutsu Kaisen.
It follows the entirety of the Shibuya Incident on October 31, 2018.
The alliance between cursed spirits and curse users forged by Mahito and Pseudo-Geto finally makes their move to seal Satoru Gojo.
